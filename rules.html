Rock — Paper — Scissors

How to play

    The player picks one of the three: Rock, Paper or Scissors.

    When the player picks his choice the npc picks one of the three randomly.

    The winner is determined by the rules below; if both choose the same, it's a draw.

Winning rules

    Rock crushes Scissors — Rock wins against Scissors.

    Scissors cut Paper — Scissors win against Paper.

    Paper covers Rock — Paper wins against Rock.

Examples

    Rock vs Scissors → Rock wins.

    Paper vs Rock → Paper wins.

    Scissors vs Scissors → Draw.
